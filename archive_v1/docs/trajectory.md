# Trajectory and Phase Representation

## Trajectory

The trajectory represents time-evolving behavior:

\[
\gamma(t) = A(t)e^{i\phi(t)}
\]

- \(A(t)\): amplitude
- \(\phi(t)\): phase

---

## Phase Synchronization

Synchronization between observed and reference behavior:

\[
\Delta\phi(t) = \phi(t) - \hat{\phi}(t)
\]

High synchronization implies strong convergence.

---

## Multi-Slice Trajectory

\[
\gamma(t) = \Phi(O_\mu, O_\eta, O_M)
\]

combining micro, meso, and macro observations.
